How It Works

Move. Unlock.
Scroll.

Three simple steps powered by real fitness data — no gamification gimmicks, just honest accountability.

01

Set Your Goal

Choose a daily activity target — steps, active calories, or workout minutes. You decide what earns your screen time.

02

Work Out

Sweat2Scroll reads your HealthKit data in real time. Every rep, every step, every calorie counts toward unlocking access.

03

Earn Your Scroll

Hit your target and the app unlocks your designated apps via Apple's FamilyControls API. No fitness, no feed.

Features

Built Different.

Not another step counter. A policy-enforced fitness gatekeeper for your digital life.

OPA Policy Engine

Open Policy Agent rules run entirely on-device via WebAssembly. No cloud server decides your access — your phone does, based on your data.

HealthKit Native

Deep integration with Apple HealthKit for heart rate, active energy, steps, and workout sessions. Real exercise only — gaming metrics won't cut it.

FamilyControls Enforcement

Uses Apple's official Screen Time framework to enforce restrictions. Apps you choose stay locked until you've earned them.

Edge Processing

All policy evaluation happens locally — sub-millisecond decisions without battery drain or privacy trade-offs.

Flexible Thresholds

Set different unlock conditions per app category. Social media costs more than podcasts. You're in control.

Activity Insights

Weekly trends show how your physical activity correlates with screen time. See the habit forming in real numbers.
